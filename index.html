Create a Custom Theme

Creating your own theme is simple and fun! First, choose a base color. This will be the color for your left-most panel. Then choose your accent color. You can use hexadecimal values as well as rgb or rgba values. When you hit ‘save’ your changes will be saved and remembered next time you use the grading tool. Choosing a pre-built theme will discard your changes and they will be lost.
